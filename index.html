"Big Six" Premier League Clubs

The top six Premier League clubs are generally in the top six spots in the league, as their name suggests. However, the order of these teams changes often. The standings are decided by the following factors, in order of importance:

  1. Points (3 for each win, 1 for each tie)
  2. Goal Differential (Goals scored - Goals conceded)
  3. Goals scored
  4. Head-to-head results
  5. Various other criteria
